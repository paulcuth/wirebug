Wirebug

Debug JavaScript remotely

  1. Create a bookmark on your remote/mobile device and point it at:
    or drag this to your bookmarks: Debug with Wirebug

  2. On your remote/mobile device, go to the page you want to debug and hit the bookmark.

  3. On your desktop machine, go to the Wirebug console, enter your Session Id and debug away.
